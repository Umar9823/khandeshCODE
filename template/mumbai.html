Tourist Destination Around Jalgaon

Jalgaon, an ideal hub, is just 1-2 hours away from several nearby tourist spots, making it a perfect base for exploration.

Tourist Places

Ajanta Caves

Ajanta Caves

With the Ellora Caves, Ajanta is one of the major tourist attractions of Maharashtra. It is about 59 kilometres (37 miles) from the city of Jalgaon, Maharashtra, India, 104 kilometres (65 miles) from the city of Aurangabad, and 350 kilometres (220 miles) east-northeast of Mumbai
